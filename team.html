Who We Are

The core team of 4Stop has been working together since little over 10 years, and we count around 700 integrations with data sources, payment services, banks and direct merchants. Integrating additional data sources and leveraging that “off the shelf” technology is truly the DNA of our company, and that is why our SLA to clients includes a guarantee to integrate any potentially missing data source in 10 business days. A practice that we are successfully executing on with our existing clients.
