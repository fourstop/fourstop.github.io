Who We Are

The core team of 4Stop has been working together since little over 10 years, and we count around 700 integrations with data sources, payment services, banks and direct merchants. Integrating additional data sources and leveraging that “off the shelf” technology is truly the DNA of our company, and that is why our SLA to clients includes a guarantee to integrate any potentially missing data source in 10 business days. A practice that we are successfully executing on with our existing clients.

Ingo Ernst

CEO, Co-Founder
Ingo is a committed Fintech entrepreneur with extensive experience in developing risk management and compliance software, implementing operational processes and managing multi-discipline teams across continents over the past 10 years. Ingo's ability to inspire his team and help businesses achive their risk goals, makes him an unbelievably strong leader.

Alvaro Kurth

CRO, Co-Founder
Alvaro is a seasoned expert of compliance on a global scale. With over 14+ years in the risk management and FinTech space, Alvaro comes with exceptional experience and knowledge to lead the 4S risk team. He is one of the core members at the forefront ensuring the 4S technology provides businesses with world-class KYC, compliance and anti-fraud solutions.

Nolan Bolusan

COO, Co-Founder

Helping companies identify issues to reduce negative impact and boost sales through use of innovative technologies and setting up proficient processes while promoting positive business culture.

Daniel Alvarado
