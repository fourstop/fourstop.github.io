Feature-Rich Verifications

Compliment your anti-fraud platform with one or all of our systems KYC features.
Geo Location IP Screening

Real-time geo location IP monitoring and alerts. Scanning from country level down to city level verifies customers location.

Device Fingerprint

Hardware device tracking and device ID verification provides further awareness of your customers intel. to mitigate fraud.

Email Verification

Review email status, age of account to profile demographics & reputational risk; including known compromised breached data.

Phone ID Verify

Geo level information with detailed phone characteristics scanned down to phone owner name and billing address.

Advanced IDV

Instantly identify shared accounts across unique and global networks through the use of risk rules numerous attributes.

Name to Address

Verifying your customers registered first and / or last name to address information, address type and length of residency.

Date of Birth

Verification of customers date of birth down to month / day / year level; which in addition, validates country’s legal age.

Social Reputation

Confirm customers through their social media networking intel against their email address and customer registrant information.
