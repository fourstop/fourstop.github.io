4Stop Wins Global Award "Risk Management Platform of the Year"
Published on October 16th, 2019. View Article on Corporate Livewire.

Founded in Germany in 2016, 4Stop is a risk management platform that aggregates trusted information, managed services, data, software and expertise to establish a next-generation all-in-one Know Your bBusiness (KYB), Know Your Customer (KYC), compliance and anti-fraud solution. Through a single API, businesses access hundreds of global KYB/ KYC data sources, data science and 4Stop's proprietary real-time anti-fraud and monitoring technology to confidently and efficiently mitigate risk worldwide.

4Stop’s KYB solution dramatically streamlines business onboarding by performing granular business underwriting in near real-time. 4Stop can quickly retrieve comprehensive data analysis on businesses’ online presence, operational performance, compliance adherence, UBO’s / Directors KYC, credit scoring and can verify business documents. Businesses obtain all the data they require to confidently accept or decline merchants and mitigate risk throughout their entire business relationship.

4Stop provides global KYC for compliance in a fail-safe, futureproof, simplistic and real-time manner. Their data hub solution encompasses one of the largest KYB/KYC data aggregations in the industry to provide true worldwide coverage, and it is currently home to over 1800 active data sources. Businesses have complete control and flexibility to enable KYB/KYC on-demand to easily adhere to their required KYC and fraud prevention needs. Paired with real-time cascading verification logic, businesses receive the best data experience possible and maximise their cost-savings.

The 4Stop anti-fraud technology provides an automated and multi-faceted rules engine that performs everything from realtime analysis with automated system actions through to granular risk monitoring with real-time intelligence. Businesses can apply risk thresholds and anti-fraud parameters per merchant, submerchant, region, type, market/ industry and date/time having full control of their risk exposure. Through the systems cascading rule logic, rule performance is maximised, dramatically improving positive transactional volumes.

4Stop’s anti-fraud system allows businesses to confidently anticipate risk and be empowered to make well-informed decisions, backed by quantifiable data, to manage regulatory obligations and fraud prevention processes, resulting in proven acceleration of businesses revenues and performance.


The Corporate LiveWire judges are impressed by the success 4Stop have achieved since its inception, becoming one of the world’s leading risk-management platforms with one of the industry’s largest KYB/ KYC data aggregations.

Judge Andrew Walsh commented: “Global fines for failure to comply with anti-money laundering regulatory requirements have exceeded one billion dollars in each of the past five years and is on course to set a new record high in 2019. As more businesses begin to realise the importance of risk management processes and Know Your Customer regulatory compliance, we expect to see 4Stop continue to go from strength-to-strength. Their sophisticated platform ensures decisions are made quickly and accurately whilst also adding value.”
