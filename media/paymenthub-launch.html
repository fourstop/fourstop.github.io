4Stop Launches Enriched Payment Monitoring & Intelligence Experience.
Published on October XXth, 2019. View Article on Newswire.

Cologne, Germany - October. XX, 2019 (NewsWire) – 4Stop (“FourStop GmbH”) a leading global KYB, KYC, compliance and fraud prevention provider announces today the launch of their enriched payment monitoring and intelligence experience. Designed through the teams consolidated 60-years risk management expertise 4Stop’s new payment hub encompasses modular architecture with innovative features for optimal payment data ingestion.


“A business can have up to 2,000 data parameters for a single transaction. If you multiply that by millions of transactions per day or sometimes even per hour, it’s tough for global companies trying to achieve instant payments under PSD2, for instance, to stay on top of compliance requirements and fraud prevention,” states Ingo Ernst, CEO, 4Stop. Additionally, in our post-breach environment, accelerating fraud rates and the birth of next-generation fraudsters combatting fraud requires sophisticated technology with an array of intelligent, automated anti-fraud tools.

The 4Stop platform is an enriched risk management solution with its harmonised world-class KYB/KYC data orchestrational hub and proprietary automated anti-fraud technology offered through a single API. “Enhancing our product from the data services offered through to its core features for premium risk mitigation and platform experience is our passion as we drive to support our evolving online financial eco-system," states Brian Daly, Head of Product Implementation and Innovation, 4Stop.

The new payment hub maximises efficiency in managing and monitoring transactions. Bringing clear insights and intelligence on transactions, volumes, payment KYC verifications, associated risk analysis through to linking commonalities across data elements and engagements in real-time. Providing a true centralised view of transactional risk to improve reaction times and empower positive transactional volumes.

The payment hub further illustrates risk score breakdowns for a clear depiction of fail rates, warning/alerts triggered and approved transaction volumes. One can easily understand customers transactional patterns from first-time engagement through to region, payment type, account associations, risk rules and KYC triggered. “Our anti-fraud engine is completely customisable to fraud prevention or compliance workflow needs. Businesses can write sets of complex rules that monitor the behaviour and transactional patterns or parameters such as phone number, email address, IP address, or cards across multiple platforms.” Adds Brian Daly

Through these modular features and reporting, businesses can easily spot trends and malicious traffic to know instantly when there is a heightened exposure to risk and vulnerability. Layered with automated anti-fraud parameters and system actions designed to be ignited at any risk threshold activated per region, merchant, sub-merchant account, payment channel or customer touchpoint.

All of this data and transactional output is processed through the new payment hub queue. With a clear separation of positive transactional volumes versus high-risk queue that focuses on traffic that is posing a threat or illicit. Businesses can easily drill down and view the transactional details, risk scoring and analysis, rules triggered, KYC output and detailed customer profiling. Managed efficiently through smart agent review tools and tracking.


“We’re excited to launch our payment hub and to bring an enriched experience to monitoring transactions. Giving the power of data-driven intelligence to bring confidence and trust to every customer and transactions processed,” states Ingo Ernst, CEO, 4Stop.
