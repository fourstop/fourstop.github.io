MuchBetter a Smart Payment App and Ewallet Sign to Integrate 4Stop’s Automated Data-Driven Merchant Underwriting Solution.
Press Release Published on November Xth, 2019. View Press Release on Newswire.

Cologne, Germany – November Xth, 2018 (NewsWire) – 4Stop (“FourStop GmbH”) a leading global provider for KYB, KYC, compliance and fraud prevention announces today its exciting client onboard of MuchBetter – a smart payment app and eWallet. This client acquisition has a driving focus to solve MuchBetter’s merchant underwriting processes through the implementation of 4Stop’s newly developed, fully customisable, automated data-driven KYB technology.

When it comes to underwriting merchants many businesses are inundated with arduous and cumbersome processes to obtain all the documentation, data and insights they require to onboard a merchant with confidence. Knowing risk is mitigated and to manage that risk assessment and compliance throughout the entire lifecycle engagement with that merchant. For many businesses, this is compacted with manual intervention for a part if not all of the onboarding flow and ongoing checks. Bringing a depth of challenges, barriers, costs and time associated with each and every underwriting case file.

4Stop’s innovative global KYB solution eliminates the cumbersome process entirely and serves up a complete end-to-end merchant underwriting solution from a single API. The solution encompasses the world’s largest KYB / KYC orchestration hub which is home to thousands of global data points, paired with real-time data performance, risk assessments, and dynamic anti-fraud technology. Enabling businesses like MuchBetter to effortlessly create customisable KYB workflows for compliance through to activating and performing data-driven due-diligence on entities and UBO’s/directors worldwide.

MuchBetter can leverage the power of data and automation to maximise their merchant underwriting with the lowest cost, time and resources possible. Ensuring they obtain all data, documents and insights required to onboard merchants with confidence and compliance. Which in turn accelerates their business performance and allows merchants quicker access to utilise the MuchBetter payment app and eWallet.


“I met Jens at PayExpo and was extremely impressed with his vision of MuchBetter. It truly is a revolutionary award-winning payment app and eWallet that allows the ability to store, send and move money securely and quickly. As with many businesses in Fintech he is eager to continue to grow his business and differentiate. I am excited to be integrating our newly developed KYB solution to solve MuchBetter’s manual merchant underwriting processes as it compliments his technology-driven payment solution with a technology-driven underwriting solution. MuchBetter can onboard merchants quicker and easier and facilitate ongoing re-checks automatically. Ensuring risk is completely mitigated all of the time.” states Christian Wheeler, VP Merchant Underwriting, 4Stop.
