Document Identity Verification
Automated. Real-Time Results. Multiple Options. One Integration.

Within our KYC services we provide an array of document identity verifications that support our evolving digital world. From fully automated identity verifications, to selfies, to interactive live chat verifications, we have everything you need to seamlessly verify customers. Increasing your KYC performance and supporting your compliance.

Stay compliant and reduce fraud through a document ID solution that can be applied at any point of a customers touchpoint.



Document ID OCR

Available in 188 countries
Fully automated ID document verification to assess quality and formating, while also analysing for scrapped data. Comes with easy upload facility, file management area, detailed analysis data on document scan and one-click downloadable reports.



Document ID Video

Available in 68 countries
Validate ID documentation in real-time through an interactive toolset that allows customers to easily upload their documents. Followed through with a live video chat verification experience with their documents and a webID agent to authenticate their identity.



Document ID Manual

Available in all global countries
As a third tier option within our back office you can easily manually review and validate ID documents or utility bill statements for quality and formating, scrapped data, and reputation. Comes with easy upload facility, file management area and downloadable reports.
