The 4Stop Solution.
Solving KYB, KYC, Compliance and
Anti-Fraud Pain Points

4Stop aggregates trusted information, managed services, data, software and expertise to establish a next-generation all-in-one KYB, KYC, compliance and anti-fraud solution.

Through one API access a complete merchant KYB onboarding solution, thousands of global data points, hundreds of global KYC data sources, harmonised data science and 4Stop's proprietary anti-fraud and monitoring technology.

Quick & Efficient Merchant Onboarding.

A recent report stated the average onboarding times to ensuring businesses and directors are properly verified for AML, compliance, and fraud prevention is 26 days and continuing to grow.

The 4Stop KYB solution dramatically reduces onboarding times, costs and manual reviews by providing all the tools required to onboard a merchant from a centralized platform.


KYB: Web Monitoring
Receive thorough assessment on merchants' online presence with risk indicators flagged, score ratings and recommendations to ensure optimal review process.


KYB: Credit
Receive comprehensive credit reports on businesses around the world. Reports encompass a clear understanding of a merchants credit score and credit limit.


KYB: Business Compliance
Receive all required business documentation including annual accounts, registered checks and reports, articles of association, and shareholder information.


KYB: UBO / Director Verification
Enhance due-diligence on UBOs, directors and shareholders with compliance watchlist screenings, address checks, document identity verifications and more.

Eliminating Multiple Integrations For KYC With Automated Verifications.

Solving KYC through multiple integrations increases costs and delay to market as it puts strain on IT Development resources, manual intervention and doesn't allow for fully automate KYC activation or harmonise data performance.

Through a single API integration instantly expand your KYC integrations and have access to hundreds of premium global KYC with real-time, on-demand activation. Paired with real-time verification processes, cascading logic and dynamic anti-fraud parameters to easily manage KYC requirements on a global scale.
